ITOM / Discovery Talent, On Demand

IT Operations Management is the engine that powers observability, automation, and event correlation across modern enterprise infrastructure. ServiceNow ITOM — spanning Discovery, Service Mapping, Health Log Analytics, and Event Management — gives IT operations teams the visibility and control they need to reduce outages and accelerate resolution. But ITOM is one of the most technically demanding areas of the ServiceNow platform, requiring engineers who understand network topology, MID Server architecture, credential management, and cloud discovery patterns. This talent is genuinely scarce. XeniaTek has cultivated a network of ITOM and Discovery engineers who have tuned Discovery schedules across thousands of CIs, built service maps for mission-critical applications, and resolved MID Server health issues in production. We place hands-on engineers who deliver results — not generalists learning on your timeline. Contract or full-time, we match your urgency.

Roles We Place

ITOM Consultant
Contract / Full-Time

Leads ServiceNow ITOM implementations across Discovery, Event Management, and Health Log Analytics. Designs the overall ITOM architecture, coordinates with infrastructure and network teams, and ensures that operational data flows accurately into the CMDB to support downstream ITSM and ITAM processes.

Discovery Engineer
Contract / Full-Time

Configures and optimizes ServiceNow Discovery for on-premises, cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), and hybrid environments. Manages credential profiles, IP ranges, discovery schedules, and classification rules. Tunes probes and sensors to maximize CI accuracy while minimizing network impact and MID Server load.

Service Mapping Specialist
Contract / Full-Time

Builds and maintains application service maps within ServiceNow that accurately represent how business-critical services depend on underlying infrastructure. Works with application owners and architects to define entry points, validate mapped CIs, and ensure service maps remain current as environments evolve.

MID Server Administrator
Contract / Full-Time

Installs, configures, and maintains ServiceNow MID Servers across segmented network environments. Manages MID Server clusters for high availability, troubleshoots connectivity and performance issues, and ensures MID Servers are properly scoped, secured, and upgraded in alignment with ServiceNow release cycles.
