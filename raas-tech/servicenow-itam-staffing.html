ITAM / SAM Talent, On Demand

IT Asset Management and Software Asset Management sit at the intersection of cost control, compliance, and operational risk. Enterprises running ServiceNow ITAM gain full visibility into hardware lifecycles, software entitlements, and vendor contracts — but only when the platform is configured and operated by professionals who understand both the tool and the discipline. Uncertified software, untracked hardware, and misaligned license positions represent millions in unnecessary spend and audit exposure. XeniaTek has built a focused network of ITAM and SAM specialists who have implemented and operated these programs across complex, multi-vendor IT environments. From initial Discovery configuration through ongoing reconciliation and renewal optimization, our consultants bring immediate, practical value. We place contractors who can run license compliance audits, administrators who manage day-to-day asset operations, and architects who design enterprise-scale asset management frameworks on the ServiceNow platform.

Roles We Place

ITAM Consultant
Contract / Full-Time

Designs and implements ServiceNow IT Asset Management programs covering hardware asset lifecycle, contract management, and asset financial tracking. Aligns platform configuration with organizational procurement processes and ITIL asset management best practices to maximize ROI on IT investments.

SAM Administrator
Contract / Full-Time

Manages the day-to-day operation of ServiceNow Software Asset Management — maintaining software models, publisher catalogs, and license entitlements. Runs reclamation workflows, generates compliance position reports, and supports vendor audit responses with accurate, platform-backed data.

Discovery & CMDB Specialist
Contract / Full-Time

Configures and tunes ServiceNow Discovery to accurately identify and classify hardware and software assets across on-premises and cloud environments. Maintains CMDB CI accuracy to support ITAM processes and ensures discovered data flows correctly into asset lifecycle and reconciliation workflows.

License Reconciliation Analyst
Contract / Full-Time

Performs detailed reconciliation between software installation data, entitlement records, and purchase history within ServiceNow SAM Pro. Identifies license gaps and over-allocations, produces effective license positions for major publishers, and supports optimization strategies to reduce software spend.
