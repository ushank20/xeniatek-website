Jason Haines
Director of Worldwide Sales & Marketing

Jason leads XeniaTek's global sales and marketing strategy, driving brand awareness and revenue growth across key markets.

In 2018, Jason was fortunate to discover "the best kept secret at ServiceNow" while at NovoScale. This, of course, was the tremendous talent of the Latin America team, which he told everyone about. NovoScale became Thirdera, and Jason was fortunate enough to have the best folks in the Western US and Latin America working for him with a simple mission: "Make ServiceNow clients happy and successful". Upon being acquired by Cognizant, Jason led all of the Americas for the ServiceNow Business Group across Manufacturing, Logistics, Energy & Utilities, and Retail.

Before that, Jason helped clients throughout the US with enterprise help desk technologies like CA-Netman and Peregrine- PNMS (and did work with Fred Luddy way back in the day). He has been in enterprise technology since 1992 and has helped clients deploy and optimize almost every computing paradigm or platform to come along.

Along this path, Jason has actually run a software company before, including sweating payroll while worrying about SOW creation and execution, recruiting, people and benefits, and, of course, trying not to break the 85% rule. (85% of the time, if you do things right and make your client happy, you'll be rehired and referred. The other 15% is being hired by bad clients or learning a crucial lesson in f-ing it up).

Jason deeply believes that being the X factor for ServiceNow clients and partners means helping folks with the right answers and the right people, regardless of the outcome for Xeniatek.

Jason spends his spare time gardening, playing music and softball, and working to strengthen his local community through involvement with organizations focused on supporting our military men and women and on anything that helps kids be kids.
