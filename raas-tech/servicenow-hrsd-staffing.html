HRSD Talent, On Demand

ServiceNow HR Service Delivery transforms the employee experience by bringing case management, knowledge, and self-service into a unified digital workspace. As organizations invest in modern HR operating models, demand for skilled HRSD practitioners has outpaced supply significantly. Successful implementations require professionals who bridge HR process expertise with deep ServiceNow technical knowledge — a rare combination that XeniaTek has spent years cultivating. Our network includes HRSD consultants who have delivered Life Events, Employee Center, and Journeys configurations for Fortune 500 HR teams. From greenfield implementations to complex upgrades and portal redesigns, we place the right specialist for each engagement stage. We screen every candidate twice before they reach you, validating both technical skills and the communication ability needed to partner effectively with HR leadership. Contract, temp-to-hire, or direct placement — we structure the engagement to fit your program.

Roles We Place

HRSD Consultant
Contract / Full-Time

Drives end-to-end ServiceNow HRSD implementations covering HR case management, Life Events, Journeys, and Employee Center Pro. Partners with HR and IT stakeholders to translate business requirements into scalable platform configurations that improve service delivery efficiency.

Employee Experience Designer
Contract / Full-Time

Designs and optimizes the employee-facing digital experience within ServiceNow — including Employee Center, service catalog design, knowledge articles, and notification workflows. Combines UX sensibility with HRSD platform expertise to drive adoption and satisfaction scores.

HR Portal Developer
Contract / Full-Time

Builds and customizes ServiceNow HR portals using Service Portal, Employee Center Pro, and UI Builder. Develops widgets, configures catalog items, and integrates HR content to create seamless self-service experiences that reduce HR inquiry volume and improve response times.

HRSD Integration Specialist
Contract / Full-Time

Connects ServiceNow HRSD with HCM systems such as Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, and Oracle HCM using IntegrationHub and REST/SOAP APIs. Ensures reliable data flow for employee onboarding, offboarding, and lifecycle events while maintaining data integrity and security standards.
