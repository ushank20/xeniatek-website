Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. Who We Are

XeniaTek (Ushank Tech doing business as XeniaTek) is a ServiceNow staffing and consulting company based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This policy explains how we handle information collected through this website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following when you use our contact or resume submission forms:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Professional information (LinkedIn profile, resume, target role)
  • Message content you voluntarily provide

We also collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, browser type, referral source) through cookies if you consent to their use.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • To match candidates with ServiceNow opportunities
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send relevant updates if you have opted in

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with client employers only with your knowledge and consent as part of the staffing process, or with service providers (such as form processing services) who are contractually required to protect it.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve site performance and, with your consent, to analyze site traffic. You may decline non-essential cookies using the banner displayed on your first visit. Essential cookies required for basic site function are always active.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@XeniaTek.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact and resume data for up to 24 months unless you request deletion earlier.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@XeniaTek.com or call +1-952-270-8453.
